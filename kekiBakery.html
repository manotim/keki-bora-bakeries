About Keki Bora Bakery

Keki Bora Bakery was founded in 2021 by the passionate baking duo, Eiden Wanjiku and Adrian Kibet. Their love for creating delicious and beautiful cakes and pastries inspired them to start this venture within the KSTVET Hotel.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring joy and sweetness to every occasion with our high-quality ingredients and unique recipes. We strive to provide our customers with an unforgettable experience, from the moment they step into our bakery to the last bite of our delectable treats.
